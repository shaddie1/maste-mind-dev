How We Work

From brief to delivery
in four steps.

01
Discovery Call

We understand your goals, constraints, users, and tech requirements. Free 45-minute consultation — no obligation, no jargon.

02
Proposal & Scoping

A clear technical proposal with scope, timeline, deliverables, and pricing. Milestone-based payments — you only pay as we deliver.

03
Build & Iterate

Regular demos, working prototypes, and full access to all files throughout the build. We keep you in the loop at every step.

04
Deliver & Support

Handover, documentation, training, and 3-month support included. Optional retainer for ongoing work.
